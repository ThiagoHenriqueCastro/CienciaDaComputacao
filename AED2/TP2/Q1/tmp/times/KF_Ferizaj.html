KF Ferizaj (Albanian: Klubi Futbollistik Ferizaj), commonly known as Ferizaj, is a professional football club based in Ferizaj, Kosovo. The club plays in the Football Superleague of Kosovo, which is the top tier of football in the country.

Ferizaj team in 1976

Privatization[edit]

On 7 July 2019, Ferizaj organized a media conference announcing that English and local investors came to the club's new leadership. Where among the investors was also the former England international striker, Brian Deane, who would own 50% of the club's shares.[1]
