KF Ballkani (Albanian: Klubi Futbollistik Ballkani), commonly known as Ballkani is a professional football club based in Suva Reka, Kosovo. The club play in the Football Superleague of Kosovo, which is the top tier of football in the country.

History[edit]

The club was established in 1947 under the name Rinia by some athletes whose purpose was to participate in various competitions and tournaments that were organized at that time. In 1952, the club was not officially registered and this year it was registered for the first time and started its organized activity and taking part in regular races. In 1965, it changed the name from KF Rinia to KF Ballkani after Suva Reka Chemical and Rubber Industry takes the ownership.

The results of the Balkan were numerous. It was the member of First League of Kosovo, in 1973–74 and then from the beginning of 1977–78, he was again a member of the first league and until 2000 he was a member of the same league. It was one of the first clubs to abandon the races from the league which was managed by the Football Association of Yugoslavia.

Ballkani played the first match against in organized league from Football Federation of Kosovo against Liria at Studençan. In the mammal of Balkani, played many prominent players from Suva Reka as Ali Elshani, Arsim Llapatinca, Avni Bytyçi, Bekim Suka, Dervish Shala, Esheref Berisha, Fillim Guraziu, Gafurr Kabashi, Hajrush Berisha, Hevzi Shalaj, Isuf Asllanaj, Isuf Kolgeci, Lulzim Kolgeci, Musli Bylykbashi, Naser Berisha, Nexhat Elshani, Osman Ramadani, Rexhep Kuçi, Salih Hoxha, Urim Bylykbashi, Visar Berisha and many others who made a valuable contribution to the affirmation of football as well as other human values and some still give their contribution to football pitches as managers and in various sports posts.[1]

Stadium[edit]

The club has played its home games at the Suva Reka City Stadium (Albanian: Stadiumi i Qytetit të Suharekës) is a multi-purpose stadium in Suva Reka, Kosovo. The stadium has a capacity of 1,500 people all seater.
