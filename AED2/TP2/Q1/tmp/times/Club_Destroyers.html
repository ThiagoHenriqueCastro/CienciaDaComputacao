Club Destroyers is a football club from Santa Cruz, Bolivia currently playing in the top-flight División de Fútbol Profesional. The club was founded September 14, 1948,[1] and they play their home games at the Estadio Ramón Tahuichi Aguilera. Destroyers competed in the top-flight Liga de Fútbol Profesional Boliviano from 1985–99 and 2005–07, and played in the first season of the Nacional B in 2011–12.[citation needed]
