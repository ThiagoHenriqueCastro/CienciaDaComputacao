Fuerza Amarilla Sporting Club is an Ecuadorian football club based in Machala, Ecuador. It was founded on 23 December 1999. They currently play in the Ecuadorian Serie A.

Honours[edit]

  • Serie B de Ecuador
Runner-up (1): 2015
